For two decades I have built brands that turn attention into commerce. For the past four years I have led the creative engine behind their campaigns on Europe’s largest fashion platform — where the consumer scrolls, taps and buys. Tommy Hilfiger and Calvin Klein both live in this work, brought to life exactly where they need to win. Move people first, and the product moves with them.
